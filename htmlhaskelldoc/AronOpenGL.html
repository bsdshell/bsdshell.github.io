sphere :: GLfloat -> [[(GLfloat, GLfloat, GLfloat)]] Source #

sphere rotates around y-axis

cut the sphere alone the xz-plane

  • The radius of the circle is \( r \cos \alpha \)
  • The circle on the xz-plane is

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} x &= r \cos \alpha \sin \beta \\ z &= r \cos \alpha \cos \beta \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

  • The center of the circle from the center of sphere is \( r \cos \alpha \sin \beta \)
  • Put all together \[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} x &= r \cos \alpha \sin \beta \\ y &= r \sin \beta \\ z &= r \cos \alpha \cos \beta \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]
